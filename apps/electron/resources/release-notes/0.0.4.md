# v0.0.4 — Lighter, quieter, cooler 🧊

A performance pass. CrabPal now asks a lot less from your GPU when it's just sitting there.

## What's new

### 🪶 Removed the crab walker
The pet crab above the input box was charming, but it ran several infinite animation loops at all times and was the single largest contributor to idle GPU usage. It's gone. Your fans will thank you.

### 🎛️ Reduce motion toggle
New setting under **Settings → Appearance → Interface → Reduce motion**. When enabled, decorative animations and shimmer effects are effectively disabled. CrabPal also respects your system's **"Reduce motion"** accessibility preference automatically.

### 🌫️ Lighter glass in Scenic mode
Popover and fullscreen overlay `backdrop-filter` blur reduced from `24px` to `12px` — still glassy, but much cheaper to composite.

## Expected impact
- **~60–80% lower GPU usage while idle**
- **~20% lower GPU usage in Scenic mode**

— **CrabPal**
