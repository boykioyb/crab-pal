# v0.0.2 — A little crab, a lot of scenes 🦀

A small, playful update. The star of the show is a new pet crab that lives above your chat input — and yes, he does more than just walk.

## What's new

### 🦀 The Crab Walker
A tiny animated crab scuttles back and forth above the input box, but now with **six different scenes** that rotate over time. Each scene has its own props, its own waypoint layout, and its own personality:

- **Beach** — stops to admire a pebble and a swaying seaweed with bubbles rising up
- **Treasure** — salutes a gold coin and a tiny sandcastle with a red flag
- **Reef** — dozes off at a starfish (💤), then nibbles on a pink clamshell
- **Garden** — sniffs a red flower, waves at a speckled mushroom
- **Music** — dances 💃 to a floating note and a thumping boombox
- **Fishing** — jumps back startled (❗) from a dangling hook, then greets a swimming fish

The walk is also much slower and calmer — no more frantic back-and-forth.

### 🔧 Small fixes
- Packaged app no longer breaks when the interceptor can't find sibling config modules.

That's it for this one. More scenes, more gestures, more crab coming soon.
